배경 영상 — Scene 1 (훅)

용도: 0~3초 배경
Extreme close-up of Korean won bills being counted, shallow depth of field, dark moody lighting. 100,000 won notes fanning out in slow motion. Cinematic dark blue-black tones. 9:16 vertical. No faces visible.

배경 영상 — Scene 2 (시중은행)

용도: 3~7초 배경
Abstract financial chart going upward with red glow, dark background. Rising interest rate visualization, red line graph climbing steeply. Numbers and percentage symbols floating. Ominous corporate mood. 9:16 vertical format.

배경 영상 — Scene 3 (정책자금)

용도: 7~12초 배경
Clean bright Korean government building exterior, modern architecture with glass and steel. Blue sky, warm sunlight hitting the facade. Camera slowly tilts up. Optimistic, trustworthy mood. Blue and white palette. 9:16 vertical format.

배경 이미지 — Scene 4 (금액 비교)

용도: 12~16초 배경
Dark minimal background with subtle green gradient glow at center. Abstract financial comparison infographic style. Split composition with warm red tones fading on left and cool green tones growing on right. No text. 9:16 vertical.

배경 영상 — Scene 5 (폐업 리스크)

용도: 16~20초 배경
Dark moody shot of closed storefront with shutters down at night. Rain on window, dimly lit street. Feeling of business closure and hardship. Desaturated blue-gray tones. 9:16 vertical format. Subtle slow camera drift.

배경 영상 — Scene 7 (CTA)

용도: 23~25초 엔딩
Clean bright blue gradient background with soft light rays converging to center. Minimal corporate ending screen. Soft white particles rising slowly. 9:16 vertical. Calm, trustworthy, professional atmosphere. Slight bokeh effect.
